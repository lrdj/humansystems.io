Future of water is future of human

Water scarcity is the biggiest problem we are facing in the 21 century. Global changes are altering where and how we get fresh water, sparkling the needs for new solutions. With design, let’s imagine the future of water together.

Tackle the biggiest challenge.

According to United Nation’s annualy report, by 2025, an estimated 1.8 billion people will live in areas plagued by water scarcity. The systems, infrastures, services and products we built before will not be relevant anymore, challenging us to design new products and services. In this 2-day workshop, we will be using speculative design methods to re-imagine our future in relation to the biggiest global problem we’re facing.

Who are we looking for?

We are looking for 20 participants to join us for the 2-day workshop to design our future together. Students, artists, engineers, designers, as long as you find passion in the topic, and you believe in creativity, join us!

What to expect

  • Team up

    You will join a team passionates with different backgrounds. We believe the future should be created by collective effort.

  • Think and do

    You will learn to use design tools to tackle big challenges. And you will design and make a prototype of your design solution.

  • Stay charged

    During the two day workshop, lunch and light refreshments will be provided.
